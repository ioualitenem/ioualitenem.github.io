Black-Scholes-Merton Model Summary

Work in progress

A paper summarizing & simplifying the Black-Scholes-Merton valuation formula.

It is a paper aimed at helping people use and/or code the formula while getting the gist of its assumptions, derivations, limitations and extensions. It also doubles as a way for me to review litterature about one of the most used models of our times and dig deeper into its strength and weaknesses in order to read on better models.

It will be posted on this website when finished.

WildCard Option Valuation

Currently being cleaned

This project was made in collaboration with my good friends : Dimitri Arcé & Édouardo Magini.

A small project on the valuation of a WildCard Option on the LGB contract launched by TMX in Fall 2021. The idea was to replicate the spot rate term structure by using the CIR++ model proposed by Brigo & Mercurio (2006) using an exact simulation (Glasserman 2003) and pricing the option using the formulas from Kane & Marcus (1986) .

A report is currently being translated and will be posted alongside a Python script.
